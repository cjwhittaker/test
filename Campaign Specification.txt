Game turn sequence



Unit Movement


Units that are contacted by enemies moving into their square
	If the unit is moving and not screening they turn to face enemy
	If the unit moving and not screening they end of their march, they ended any forced march, but they retain their existing route
	If the unit is screening they try to withdraw
	If halted and not in contact and not contacted in the rear retain current facing
	If halted and not in contact and contacted in the rear turn to face
	If halted and not in contact and no current facing turn to face
	If the unit is already in contact do nothing
